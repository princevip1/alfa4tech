Today, AMK Trading helps business start-ups, mid-sized companies and global brands to transform processes in ingenious ways.

Our story began in 2013 with just four people - two programmer and two business professional. Between them, they had experience working for the likes of IBM. More importantly, they had a vision to help companies to use software to solve challenges and change the fortunes of their businesses.

Since the early days, we've grown steadily and now employ more than 50 people in the all over the WORLD. We've won awards and now provide bespoke software and web development services to a wide range of organisations.

Our values

1. CODE QUALITY

We create high-quality, well-structured and comprehensible code that enables easy future development of any solution. Thanks to its transparency, the software can easily be maintained and upgraded either by ourselves or others.

2. DOMAIN DRIVEN DESIGN

Before we start coding your solution, we gain a detailed understanding of how your company operates in your industry, so we can develop an accurate solution. As a result, our software not only meets your needs but is powerful enough to face challenges from your competitors.

3. TRANSPARENCY

We welcome you as part of the team, working with us to develop a powerful software solution tailored to your company. We listen closely and work in partnership with you - so everyone stays on the same page and is focused on getting the right result.

4. EASY MANAGEMENT

Our approach to software development, which is a hybrid of Agile and other methodologies, allows us to manage both our team and your project in an efficient way. Keeping everyone updated on progress and building a transparent work environment speeds up the entire process of software development.

5. COMMUNICATION

We care about developing long-term business relationships with our clients and build a strong rapport with them. We walk with you through the whole process and are there to support you with ongoing advice and maintenance. Our goal is for your business to prosper, year after year.

6. ENGAGEMENT AND RESPONSIBILITY

We are totally committed to every project. We guarantee that the team assigned to your project will be dedicated to the task, staying focussed and engaged with the needs of your business. We never outsource your projects to third parties.
